Regulations

The essence of the game is as follows: On the field, on which "cubes" are located in random order, there are colored blocks. Cubes are removed from the field by hovering the cursor over the cube and then clicking the left mouse button.

- red cube: 3 points

- blue cube: 2 points

- green cube: 1 points
